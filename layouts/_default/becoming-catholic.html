Who This Is For

OCIA may be the right next step.

OCIA is the usual path for adults who are discerning entrance into the Catholic Church or completing their Sacraments of Initiation.

You have never been baptized.

The Church will help you prepare for Baptism, Confirmation, and First Holy Communion.

You were baptized in another Christian tradition.

The parish can help you learn Catholic teaching and prepare to be received into full communion.

You were baptized Catholic but have not received all your Sacraments.

The parish can help you prepare for Confession, Confirmation, and Holy Communion as needed.

You are returning after time away.

Welcome home. The parish office can help you find the right next step, including Confession, Mass, formation, or a conversation with Father.
