The sacraments are at the heart of Catholic life. They are gifts of grace given by Christ to His Church.

This page will help parishioners and visitors know who to contact and what steps to take.

## Sacraments

- Baptism
- Confession
- Eucharist
- Confirmation
- Marriage
- Anointing of the Sick
- Funerals
