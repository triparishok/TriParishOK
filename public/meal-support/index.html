Meal Support Ministry

When someone in our parish family is walking through illness, grief, childbirth, recovery, or a heavy season, a simple meal can be a real act of love.

Our Meal Support Ministry helps coordinate meals, freezer meals, grocery drop-offs, delivery cards, and other practical help when there is a need.

You may request support for yourself, nominate someone who may need help, or sign up to volunteer. All requests are reviewed before anything is shared. We do not publish private family or medical details.

If the need is urgent or sacramental, please contact the parish office directly.
