Parents & Families

The home is the first school of faith.

The Church teaches that parents have the first responsibility for the education of their children in the faith. Parish formation supports that mission; it does not replace it.

Attend Sunday Mass.

The Mass is the center of Catholic life. Regular Sunday worship forms the whole family.

Pray at home.

Simple habits like grace before meals, bedtime prayers, the Rosary, and Scripture reading help children grow in faith.

Prepare for the Sacraments.

Sacramental preparation should include parish formation, family prayer, and faithful participation in Mass.

Live the liturgical year.

Advent, Christmas, Lent, Easter, feast days, and saint days help families mark time with Christ and His Church.
