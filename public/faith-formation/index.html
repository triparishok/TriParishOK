Practical Details

What families should know.

Specific class times, registration windows, and sacramental requirements are confirmed through the parish office and current parish notices.

Who should contact the parish?

Families seeking Religious Education, sacramental preparation, Baptism for a child, First Confession, First Holy Communion, Confirmation, or help returning to formation should contact the parish office.

What information should families be ready to share?

The parish may need the child’s name, age or grade, parish connection, sacramental history, parent contact information, and any preparation already completed.

Where are current schedules and deadlines posted?

Current parish notices, volunteer needs, registration updates, and schedule changes are shared through the weekly bulletin and parish office.
