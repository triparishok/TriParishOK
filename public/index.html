Special Mass St. Ann · 10:30 AM

Archbishop Coakley, President of the USCCB, will celebrate Mass at St. Ann

This is a special honor for the whole tri-parish community. Archbishop Paul S. Coakley, Archbishop of Oklahoma City and President of the United States Conference of Catholic Bishops, will celebrate Mass with us on Sunday, August 2 at 10:30 AM at St. Ann in Elgin. A meal will follow Mass. Because this is one tri-parish celebration, the regular Sunday Mass schedule changes that day.
