Safe Environment

Safe Environment & Reporting Abuse

The Church has a serious duty to protect children and vulnerable adults. These reporting resources are provided for safety, accountability, and care for victims.

Emergency

Immediate danger

If a child or vulnerable adult is in immediate danger, call 911.

Call 911

Civil Reporting

Oklahoma DHS Statewide Abuse Hotline

To report known or suspected abuse or neglect of a child or vulnerable adult, call the Oklahoma DHS Statewide Abuse Hotline.

Call 1-800-522-3511

Archdiocese

Archdiocesan Pastoral Response Hotline

For abuse of a minor or vulnerable adult, past or present, by clergy or other Archdiocesan personnel, contact the Archdiocesan Pastoral Response Hotline.

Call 405-720-9878

Official Resources

For training, policies, reporting information, and Archdiocesan resources, visit the official Safe Environment pages.
