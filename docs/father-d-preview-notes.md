# Father D. Preview Notes

## Purpose

This is a draft tri-parish website for St. Ann, Our Lady of Perpetual Help, and Mother of Sorrows.

The goal is to make parish information easier to find, especially for:
- Mass and Confession times
- Current bulletin notices
- Parish locations and directions
- Sacraments
- Faith Formation
- Becoming Catholic / OCIA
- Contacting the parish office
- Safe Environment and reporting information
