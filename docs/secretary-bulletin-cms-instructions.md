# Updating the Weekly Bulletin in Pages CMS

## Weekly steps

1. Open Pages CMS.
2. Choose the tri-parish website repository.
3. Click **Weekly Bulletin**.
4. Upload the new bulletin PDF.
5. Update the bulletin date.
6. Update the number of pages.
7. Add up to three key notices from the bulletin.
8. Click **Save**.

That is all.

## What appears on the website

The information entered here updates:

- the Bulletins page
- the homepage “Important This Week” cards

## What to include as key notices

Use up to three of the most important items from the current bulletin.

Good choices include:

- schedule changes
- Holy Day Mass times
- parish meals or major events
- Religious Education updates
- volunteer needs
- office closures
- sacramental preparation notices

If there is a schedule change, it should almost always be one of the key notices.

## Parish field

Use the parish field only if the notice belongs to one parish.

Examples:

- OLPH lawn care need → choose OLPH
- St. Ann event → choose St. Ann
- tri-parish announcement → leave parish blank

## Parish color field

If a parish is selected, choose the matching parish color:

- St. Ann → St. Ann
- OLPH → OLPH
- Mother of Sorrows → Mother of Sorrows

If no parish is selected, leave parish color blank.

## Safety note

The bulletin remains the source of truth. The website notices are only a short readable summary.
