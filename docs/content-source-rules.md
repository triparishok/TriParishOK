# Content Source Rules

The weekly parish bulletin is the source of truth for parish schedules, contact details, announcements, and office information.

If the current website and the bulletin do not match, use the bulletin.

Before launch, Father D. or the parish office should confirm:
- Mass times
- Confession times
- Adoration times
- Rosary times
- Office contact information
- Sacramental emergency guidance
- Online giving links
- Registration and form links

The website should never rely only on a PDF for key information. Mass times, Confession times, office contact details, and urgent updates should also appear as regular website text.
