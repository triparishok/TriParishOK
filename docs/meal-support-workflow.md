# Meal Support Ministry Workflow

## Public goal

Allow parishioners to:
- Request meal support
- Nominate someone for support
- Volunteer to help

## Privacy rules

- Do not publish a family’s need without consent.
- Do not collect detailed medical history.
- Do not post addresses publicly.
- Do not post children’s names.
- Do not share raw Google Sheet access widely.
- Keep all form responses in parish-owned Google Workspace.
- Limit access to the parish office and approved ministry coordinator.

## Form setup

Use Google Forms in parish-owned Google Workspace.

Recommended forms:
1. Request Support
2. Nominate Someone
3. Volunteer to Help

## Approval needed

Father D. or the parish office should approve:
- Public page wording
- Form questions
- Who receives notifications
- Who may access response sheets
- Whether public meal signups are allowed
